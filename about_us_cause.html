About Us

Body Magicians is a campaigning organization of volunteers who believe that our body is unique and has its own beauty. We find its important to fight against image misconceptions. Our body image is how we perceive ourselves; how we feel inside and out. There is a direct relationship between our body images and our self esteems. Our goal is to help everyone feel great about themselves and have a healthy self esteem. Only in that case we will live in a healthy society and most importantly we will be able to save millions of lives.
